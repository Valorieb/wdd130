Splash White Water Rafting

At Splash White Water Rafting, we don’t just navigate rivers—we create unforgettable adventures. From heart-pounding rapids to breathtaking scenic floats, our expert guides are here to lead you through the wild beauty of the great outdoors. Whether you're seeking an adrenaline rush or a peaceful escape, your next great adventure starts here!

History

Splash White Water Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of thrill-seekers who believed that the best way to experience the great outdoors was by riding the rapids. What started as a small operation with just a few rafts and a passion for adventure quickly grew into one of the most trusted names in white water rafting.

From the roaring Class IV rapids to the gentle scenic floats, we have spent over two decades guiding adventurers of all skill levels through some of the most breathtaking rivers. Our expert team is made up of seasoned river guides who share a love for the water and a commitment to safety.
