Our Purpose

Splash White Water Rafting provides thrilling and unforgettable rafting adventures for all skill levels, offering guided trips through exhilarating rapids and breathtaking landscapes.

Our mission is to create safe, exciting, and memorable experiences that bring people closer to nature and the thrill of the river. Whether you're a beginner looking for a fun introduction to rafting or an experienced paddler seeking the ultimate challenge, we have the perfect adventure for you.

What We Offer

Adventures:

  • Half-Day Adventure – A fun and exciting introduction to white water rafting, perfect for beginners.
  • Full-Day Adventure – A thrilling full-day trip with more rapids and breathtaking scenery.
  • Multi-Day Adventure – A multi-day rafting expedition for those seeking the ultimate outdoor experience.
  • Los Dientes del Diablo – An intense, expert-level ride through some of the most challenging rapids.

    The experience of a lifetime:

  • Experienced Guides – Our professional guides ensure a safe and exhilarating adventure.
  • Stunning River Landscapes – Enjoy breathtaking views while riding the rapids.
  • Gear Rental – No need to bring your own gear. We have everything you need ready to go!
